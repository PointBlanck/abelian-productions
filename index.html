Raising the Bar

Abelian Productions is a multi-sector production company that refuses to be categorized and whose only goal is innovation and improvement. From drones, to gadgets, to movies & games and beyond, our team is dedicated to crafting/providing quality products/services with one individual in mind... YOU.

Projects of Passion

We love creating

The company's founding members created it with one goal in mind: Change the status quo. We believe that letting people choose the projects they want to contribute to is crucial to the final outcome. That is precisely why we pick ambitious and talented people with give them the tools they need to produce the masterpieces they envision. You can check out our previous projects of ours for a more palpable idea of our experience and dedication.

Redifining Innovation

Putting Life back in Animation

Receiving Feedback and Custom Project Requests

We base our values on a foundation of cooperation and feedback

Although our company has a pretty limited supply of products and services, our extensive business network allows for quick and versatile collaborations between our partners and so we are capable of undertaking any custom project our customers might request. Our team of experts remains always up-to-date about the latest and greatest advances in any tech sector. That is possible through our company's policy of constant prototyping which allows for creativity and learning of new technologies, through application and optimization.

Our client outreach team makes sure that we know what they want and use their feedback to make better products and make access to our services easier. If you have used one of our products or services do not hesitatet to contact us. We'll be happy to hear your thoughts!
