A Consumer's Company

Abelian Productions is the result of the hard work of a dedicated group of friends. For years we have felt that the quality of products and services dominating every sector of the market were solely focused on the maximization of profits and not consumer/user satisfaction. In Abelian Productions we aim to change that!

We want to be proud of the impact we have on the world. That is the ideology we follow in every step of the production process. Whether we're making a user-oriented, cool, new gadget or we are applying our creativity virtually on a screen, you can count on the fact that we do so with you in mind!

Meet our People!

Theodosios D.K. Kavalagios

CEO & Technical Lead

Nothing is impossible. It just requires time and effort.

I am a straight-to-the-point, consise talker and a curious mind by nature. I believe that anyone, no matter their nature, can do great things if they stay consistent and know how to think critically. I like to see the best in people, and push them to be better every day. Our company is just what happens when a few determined persons decide to pursue their potential together.
